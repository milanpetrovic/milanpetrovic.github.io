Dual-Portal · B2B Fleet · Shopify

Enterprise Dual-Channel Architecture

Engineered a dual-channel enterprise architecture for publicly traded powersports manufacturer Massimo Motor (NASDAQ: MAMO). The solution bridges a high-volume Shopify storefront for parts and accessories with a purpose-built Ekho Dealer portal to handle high-ticket vehicle compliance and B2B fleet sales. Unified Global DTC Infrastructure

Delivered a unified global DTC architecture for German manufacturer LA SIESTA, serving over 100 countries and 10+ currencies from a single Shopify storefront. To drive conversions for highly considered purchases, we embedded custom SPA product quizzes directly into the navigation architecture. Headless SPA · Multi-Market DTC · Shopify Plus

Localized Retail Discovery Architecture

Architected a multi-market DTC experience for Planted, a Swiss food-tech B Corp, supporting localization across five languages with a primary focus on the Swiss market. The core innovation is a custom Single Page Application (SPA) store locator deployed on a dedicated subdomain. This decoupled SPA scales across 7+ countries and 13+ retail chains without impacting Shopify performance. The build also features a custom cart widget calculating real-time CO2 and water savings per checkout.
